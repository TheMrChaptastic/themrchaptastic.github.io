About Me:

I began coding in high school by making scripts to be used with programs to automate repetitive video game inputs or tasks. In 2017 I began learning Java to make scripts for Old School Runescape Bots to level up accounts and complete in-game tasks. I made multiple script projects for these programs and became more comfortable with For Loops, While Loops and Logic Gates.

At the beginning of 2022 I enrolled in TrueCoders as part of their Full Stack Software Development Program. It was a wonderful experience that helped me develop my skills and taught me about the tools I could utilize to make my dream projects a reality.

I am now a .Net Developer with fundamental knowledge of development and testing and am always eager to learn more and improve on my development skills.
